1. Only a team of two players can take part in this game.

2. Each player of the team has to act and find 5 words.

3. Each word will be displayed for 60 seconds.

4. The game will begin only when the acting player presses the START/ Skip(visible only for acting player).Use the same button to skip any difficult word to act.

5. The first connected player acts the word for the second connected player to guess.

6. The first connected player continues to act for 5 words.
7. Points are rewarded for finding the correct word.Once the game ends ,the joint score is displayed.

8. Once the joint score is displayed,new game eventually begins in next 15 seconds.

9. The roles of the players will be swapped among them for every new game.

10. The game moves on until any one player wishes to leave voluntarily.
